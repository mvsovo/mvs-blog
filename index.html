Wejun Li

I am an HCI researcher and am now a second-year master student at International Design Institute, School of Computer Science and Technology of Zhejiang University, advised by Lingyun Sun and Shi Chen.

I do Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) and Computer-Supported Cooperative Work (CSCW) research that focuses on well-being, health, and social media. I explore how technology and social media affect relationships. I am also curious about how we can design and build new systems, or curate existing ones to better support people's well-being and health.

Outside of research, you can find me cycling in the nearest mountain.
