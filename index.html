Weijun Li

I am an HCI researcher and am now a last-year master student at International Design Institute, School of Computer Science and Technology of Zhejiang University, advised by Lingyun Sun and Shi Chen.

I do Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) and Computer-Supported Cooperative Work (CSCW) research that focuses on well-being, privacy, and identity within digital contexts. I am also curious about how we can design and build new responsible and wellbeing-oriented socio-technical systems, or curate existing ones to better support the well-being of a broader demographic.

Outside of research, you can find me cycling in the nearest mountain. My friends and I also started an encrypted email,METAMAIL and received several rounds of fundraising investment.
